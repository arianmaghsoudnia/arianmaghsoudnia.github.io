Ansys Fluent

CFD analysis, Modelling

Many factors including air circulation around the window, the amount to which the gap between the windows has been vacuumed and the shape of gaps, and the distance between the panes can play important roles in the total heat transfer of multiple pane windows. This project uses a commercial CFD code to determine the most efficient tilting angle of the windows and also the optimum distance between the panes.
